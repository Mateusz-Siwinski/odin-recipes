Bigos (Polish Hunter's Stew)

Bigos is a great idea for delicious dinner for the whole family.
With the addition of aromatic mushrooms will be perfect as an exquisite dish.

Ingredients

  • White cabbage 500 g
  • Sauerkraut 500 g
  • Onion 200 g 2 large pieces
  • Raw pork ham 400 g
  • Tomato puree without salt 50 g
  • Dried plums 100 g
  • Dried mushrooms 40 g
  • Rapeseed oil 120 ml
  • Hunter sausage 300 g

Directions

  1. Finely chop the white cabbage.
  2. Chop sauerkraut.
  3. Place sauerkraut in a pot, pour 500 ml of water and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Dice the pork ham.
  5. Peel the onion and dice it.
  6. Heat the rapeseed oil in a pot and fry the ham with onion, pour 500 ml of water, add soy sauce, juniper, allspice and bay leaf, simmer covered for 30 minutes.
  7. Add the white cabbage to the pot and cook for 20 minutes.
  8. Add sauerkraut, tomato puree, season with salt and pepper.
  9. Cook for 15 minutes.
  10. Cut the sausage into slices.
  11. Add the sausage to the pot along with the prunes and wild mushrooms.
  12. Stew bigos for 20 minutes.
  13. Serve with boiled potatoes or fresh rye bread.
