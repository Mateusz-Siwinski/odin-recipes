Pierogi with Cottage Cheese (Polish Dumplings)

Dumplings with cottage cheese are a great proposition for a delicious sweet dinner for the whole family.
Pierogi can also be served with fresh or frozen fruit.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour 500 g
  • cottage cheese 300 g
  • sour cream 30% fat 100 g
  • Sugar 200 g
  • Butter 200 g
  • 2 Eggs

Directions

  1. Sift wheat flour into a bowl, pour warm water and knead a smooth dough.
  2. Leave for 30 minutes.
  3. Put the cottage cheese in a bowl, add sugar, sour cream and eggs, mix thoroughly.
  4. Roll out the dough to a thickness of 0.5 cm and cut circles with the pastry edge.
  5. Place a spoonful of curd filling on each piece of dough and seal the edges.
  6. Boil water in a pot and cook dumplings in batches until they float.
  7. This should take about 5-6 minutes.
  8. Serve the dumplings on a plate with the addition of butter and gingerbread spices.
